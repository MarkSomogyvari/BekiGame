# Game Report: Collaborative Monsoon Management
**ID:** BG-2026-001 | **Date:** 2026-05-16

## 🌊 Scenario Overview
The game began during a **Monsoon** season characterized by high river levels and saturation. The primary challenge emerged when an **Extreme Event** card introduced **Wildlife Conflict**: flooding in the highlands forced elephant herds into community croplands, creating a secondary crisis alongside the rising water.

## 🛠️ Team Strategies

### Government Team (Team A)
The government focused on **Integrated Reservoir Management**. Their initial plan prioritized dam release schedules and embankment strengthening. Following feedback, they incorporated an **Early Warning System** specifically designed for downstream communities to ensure they have enough time to protect assets before water levels peak.

### Community Team (Team B)
The community team took a proactive approach to the wildlife issue. They consulted a **Social Scientist** (Power Card) to understand how to manage human-elephant conflict without violence. Their final proposal focused on **Bio-fencing** (using natural deterrents) and diversifying crops to include species that elephants are less likely to target.

## 💬 Key Interactions
*   **The Power Room:** The community team's use of a Social Scientist was a turning point, providing the technical backing needed for their bio-fencing strategy.
*   **The Exchange:** The Government asked a critical question about the compensation fund's logistics, which forced the Community to refine their financial model.
*   ** Solidarity:** The Community successfully lobbied for a 48-hour notice on dam releases, which the Government integrated into their final protocol.

## 🏁 Final Outcome
**Result:** ✅ **SUCCESSFUL_COLLABORATION**

**Facilitator's Note:** This session was a model for BekiGame. The Government demonstrated a high degree of inclusion, adapting their master plan based on direct community feedback. The use of expert consultation was efficient and led to a technically sound, socially acceptable solution.
